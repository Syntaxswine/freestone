# The Durham seam ladder (correlation spine for the bed model)

*2026-07-10 · verified before the bed model leans on it (honest-dating law applies to
stratigraphy too). The marginalia in the Victorian logs (LM, Md, HM, BLM, TGT…) are
BGS geologists' seam correlations — this ladder is what they abbreviate.*

Marginalia reading hypothesis (to be tested by the bed-model fit, not assumed): pairs
like "95′ / 85/LM" beside a coal in the Elvet log read as *depth 95 ft; seam elevation
85 ft OD; correlated Low Main* (surface c. 180 ft OD − 95 ft = 85 ft OD — the
arithmetic closes). Unresolvable abbreviations stay verbatim in the transcriptions.
